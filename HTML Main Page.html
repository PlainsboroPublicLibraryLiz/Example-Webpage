Welcome to my Webpage about Snakes!

I love snakes. Snakes are amazing and cool. Here is a list of some reasons why I love snakes:

  • They are not slimy, they are soft!
  • They like to cuddle with you.
  • They only need to be fed once every week or every other week depending on the type of snake.

There are also many types of snakes. Here is a table listing a few of my favorite types of snakes, and their differences.

Type of Snake Longevity (in captivity) Length at full growth Tempement
Ball Python 20-25 years 3-5 feet One of the most docile, will not strike unless attacked.
Milk Snake 15-20 years 4 feet Nervous snakes, can strike when young if not careful.
Corn Snake 10-15 years 2-3 feet Very calm, seldom bites unless provoked.
Garder Snake 10 years 3 feet or less Nervous snakes, but can calm down after years in captivity.
